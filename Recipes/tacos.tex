\food{Tacos de Pollo}
\preptime{10 minutes}
\cooktime{15 minutes}
\people{4-6}

\recipe{
    \unit{1}{kg} & Chicken Thighs\\
    \unit[1]{tsp} & Salt\\
    \unit[1]{tsp} & Pepper\\
    \unit[1]{tsp} & Cumin\\
    \unit[1]{tsp} & Ground Coriander\\
    \unit[1]{tsp} & Chilli Powder\\
    \unit[1]{tsp} & Cayenne Pepper\\
    \unit[1]{tsp} & Paprika\\
}{
    \item Mix all spices together in a small bowl.
    \item Rub the spice mix onto the chicken thighs.
    \item Place chicken thighs in a hot pan and cook until golden brown.
    \item Shred the chicken thighs with a fork (I often enjoy chunky pieces if I use breast).
    \item Serve with tortillas, pico de gallo, guacamole, and sour cream. I also like to add some cheese, tabasco, and lime.
}
